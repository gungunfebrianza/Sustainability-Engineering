Critical Priorities

Actionable Problems

  • Supply-Demand Imbalance

    Oversupply of low-quality credits, weak demand, price suppression

  • Corporate Greenwashing

    Risks of misleading claims, avoidance of actual emission cuts

  • Technology Gaps

    MRV challenges, digital infrastructure limitations, verification costs

Strategic Insights

Quadrant I Focus

Critical priorities require immediate attention through international cooperation and regulatory reform. These foundational issues must be addressed for the carbon market to function effectively.

Quadrant IV Opportunities

Actionable problems offer the best opportunities for near-term improvements. These can be addressed through market mechanisms, technology solutions, and corporate accountability measures.

Long-Term Strategy

Governance challenges require systemic changes but offer the potential for transformative impact. Manageable issues should be monitored but don't require significant resources.
